 For Normal Weight Persons (Goal: Maintenance & Health)

Focus on a balanced routine that incorporates aerobic, strength, and flexibility training. 
Cardiovascular Health:
Brisk Walking or Jogging: Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate activity weekly.
Jumping Rope: A high-intensity option for those with good joint health.
Strength and Tone:
Include activities like Yoga or Pilates to improve core strength and balance.
Perform full-body strength training (e.g., lunges, planks) at least 2 days per week.
Activity Levels: Supplement planned exercise by reducing sitting time and increasing light movements like gardening or household chores.
